Welcome to WhiteBored!

A place where you can share your Boredom with the world

About WhiteBored

WhiteBored is a simulation of the communal whiteboards that you pass by in hallways or in lounges, on which people can draw or write something to leave for others to see and perhaps respond to and build upon. It is not meant to be a super fancy drawing app -- many of those already exist. Thus (for now), you are supplied with 7 marker colors and an eraser, each with a fixed size. But the creativity you supply for yourself is endless!

What's Next
